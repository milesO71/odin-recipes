Taco Recipe

This is the recipe for a very complex taco suitable for only the most refined of palates. Ingredients are as follows:

  • Taco Shells
  • Ground Beef
  • Lettuce
  • Taco Sauce
  • Cheese

To make this taco, you must follow these steps:
  1. Buy the materials
  2. Cook the ground beef
  3. Take the ground beef off the pan
  4. Put the ground beef in the taco shell
  5. Put the other stuff in the taco shell
