Burger Recipe

This is the recipe for a very complex burger suitable for only the most refined of palates. Ingredients are as follows:

  • Buns
  • Beef Patty
  • Lettuce
  • Ketchup
  • Cheese

To make this burger, you must follow these steps:
  1. Buy the materials
  2. Cook the burger
  3. Take the burger off the grill
  4. Put the burger in the bun
  5. Put the other stuff in the bun
